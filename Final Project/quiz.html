WHAT IS DECOY EFFECT?

When we are choosing between two alternatives, the addition of a third, less attractive option (the decoy) can influence our perception of the original two choices. Decoys completely inferior to one option (the target, in this case Venti) but only partially inferior to the other (the competitor, in this case Tall).
